Stadium Naming Rights

 

Stadium Naming Rights

Elise M. Neils,
Principal,
AbsoluteBusiness, LLC, Milwaukee, WI

{Presentation originally given at the State Bar of Wisconsin Sports and Entertainment Law Section Meeting, Lake Geneva, WI, May 2, 2001.}

ELISE NEILS is a Principal with AbsoluteBusiness LLC and specializes in valuing businesses, sports franchises, and other tangible assets including brand names, securities, and software and in relation to business strategy, tax assessments, fairness opinions, litigation, mergers and acquisitions. Prior to her current position, she worked for a large regional investment banking firm and other appraisal firms. She also practiced corporate and securities law. Elise holds a J.D. from Marquette University Law School and a Bachelor of Business Administration in Finance from the University of Wisconsin - Milwaukee. She has successfully passed the American Society of Appraisers Business Valuation Advancement Examination; is a member of the State Bar of Wisconsin; the National Association of Securities Dealers Board of Arbitrators; and the Alumni Board of the National Sports Law Institute. She has spoken extensively on valuation topics and most recently testified at an SEC hearing on auditor independence at Pace University in New York City.
