National Hockey League Final Standings Points

The NHL determines its final standings by points, rather than just by winning percent.  This follows from the fact that tie games are still allowed, unlike any other pro sport:

Prior to 1998-99 season, 2 points for a win, 1 for a tie.
Starting with the 1998-99 season added one point for a loss in overtime.

The entries are all from NHL.com's "History" area, in MSExcel format.  So, use your "Save Target As" command to save them to your hard disk.
